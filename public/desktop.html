Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Paxxage Studio built with?

Paxxage uses the Electron.js app to build and compile a fast and light-weight desktop application. Forge and OpenSSL are the integrated crypto platforms. NeDB is used to manage your keys and ciphers.

Why use RSA key pairs for login?

Key pairs allow you to anonymously and securely sign into services without email, username, password, or any other identifying marker. There is no email provider monitoring the services you sign up for. You have freedom to use internet services with anonymity.

Paxxage is a key wallet. How are my keys kept safe?

That is up to you. We recommend that you keep your Paxxage data split between two FIPS 140-3 USB encrypted storage devices. We built the Desktop app to do this natively. The security of any protocol has as much, or more, to do with human usage as it does the cryptographic protocols used.

Why RSA 4096 and 8192 bit encryption when the world is still using RSA 2048.

1) RSA 2048 is only rated to 2030. Our goal is that your crypto lasts your lifetime.
2) Moore's Law is placing the growth of processing speed on an exponential curve.
3) Quantum computing might impact crypto so let's not make it too easy.
