Secure and anonymous

Remcasts uses anonymous secure logins.

No username, no password, no email links.
Just an alias and a key.

Your key gives you access to your account.
It cannot be recovered if it is lost.

This key is generated in your browser (you can disconnect from the internet).

However, we strongly recommend you use a tool like OpenSSL to generate your own. It is generally best not to trust any website that provides your keys for you.
